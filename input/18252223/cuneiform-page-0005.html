Metabolic Profiling

The determination of both acylcarnitines and amino acids were performed on a Turbo U ion source (T = 100'C) of an Applied Biosystems/MDS-SCIEX API4000 triple quadropole tandem mass spectrometer (MSlMS) instrument (Concord, Ontario, Canada). Samples were infused into the MS/MS at a flow of mobile phase (100 pl/min) with an Agilent 1100 HPLC system (Waldbronn, Ger- many). We derivatized Whole blood-spot samples with butanolic- HCI to form butyl-esters, the acylcarnitines were determined by precursor scan of 85.1 amu, and the amino acids were determined by neutral total loss of 102 amu. The levels of each acylcarnitine and amino acid in pmol/I whole blood were determined against the respective deuterated stable isotope with the Analyst software, Chemoview (Applied Biosystems) and were compared against a normal age-matched population.
