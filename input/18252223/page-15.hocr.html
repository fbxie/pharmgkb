Figure 5. Schematic Representation of the Xp11.22 Region Showing the Duplications

(A) Indicated from top to bottom: the loca- tion of the qPCR primer sets (1-20), geno- mic BAC or PAC clones, and annotated genes present in the interval composed of the six duplications. This region is repre- sented for each family by the arrows indi- cating the genomic clones from the X array with their corresponding logz ratios. Clones with aberrant ratios are in green; those with ratios in the normal interval are open. For each family, the mapped duplicated region, deﬁned by qPCR, is given as solid blue bars above these clones, indicating that the primer sets within these bars yielded relative copy numbers of ~2.0 whereas the primer pairs outside (indicated with the gray bars) gave values ~1.0.
